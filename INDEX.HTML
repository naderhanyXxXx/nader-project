A VIBRANT DESTINATION IN DOWNTOWN LA

Surrounded by iconic art, theatre, and music institutions, Conrad Los Angeles channels the bold originality, creative innovation and dynamic energy that fuels Downtown LA s newest cultural corridor.

ROOMS & SUITES

Spanning across 25 floors, unwind in one of our 305 luxury guest rooms that feature spectacular views of the Walt Disney Concert hall and Downtown LA.

DINING & DRINKS

Discover contemporary dining in one of our on-site restaurants and bars, including our Signature Restaurant led by a celebrity chef.

EVENTS AT CONRAD LOS ANGELES

Host an event to remember in one of our inspiring spaces, including two ballrooms and meeting rooms with state-of-the-art technology and dedicated event specialists.

SPA

Take a moment to relax in Conrad Spa and explore an infrared sauna, wave room, a next-generation thermal bath, and a beauty salon with rejuvenating treatments.
